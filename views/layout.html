Instructions
Goal: Be the player with the lowest score possible!
- Play 3 tiles.
- Your score that round is the sum of those 3 numbers.
- Gray tiles and your own tiles are worth the numbers on them.
- Using your opponent's tiles gives you the opposite of the numbers on them.
- You can only use one of your opponent's tiles per turn.
- Game ends when all gray tiles are used up.
